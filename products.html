PRODUCTS

The detector is a critical, key component in any X-ray imaging system. At the heart of all XCounter detectors is direct conversion CdTe photon counting technology, which provides unparalleled image quality. All of our products deliver market leading MTF and DQE. We provide two families of Flite products; fixed (FX series) and scanning (X series), then we have the smaller PDT-25DE product and the super fast Hydra FX-series. In addition to our off the shelf product we provide custom solutions for OEM.

XC-FLITE X1

XC-FLITE X-Series

The smallest self-scanning detector which makes it useful for Dental applications and Scientific laboratory work.

XC-FLITE X2

XC-FLITE X-Series

A perfect balance for scanning performance and cost, providing a smaller active scanned area than the XC-Flite X3, this detector works well in DUPRO applications and larger Scientific requirements.

XC-FLITE X3

XC-FLITE X-Series

The longest self-scanning Flite series detector suitable for DUPRO applications where long detector length is needed or Security applications where a fast scan is needed of the whole object.

XC-FLITE FX1

XC-FLITE FX-Series

The smallest fixed Flite series detector with a broad range of applications, in particular Weld Integrity where the detector can be dropped into an existing mechanical scanning system and Field Inspection applications where portability and robustness is important.

XC-FLITE FX2

XC-FLITE FX-Series

The FX2 is suitable for Scientific applications and some Dental applications.

XC-FLITE FX3

XC-FLITE FX-Series

For imaging of long length applications such as C-arm Medical scanning or fixed above a conveyor system such as Food quality control.

PDT25-DE

Small area detector

The most compact and robust of our detectors making the PD25T-DE perfect for Field Inspection and NDT applications such as Weld Integrity.

OEM Products

Customized Detectors

With the same core technology in all our products, we are able to custom any of the above detectors to suit your system requirements.

Hydra FX Series

Ultra Fast Detectors

The longest length detector at 50cm and the fastest detector which can detect sample passing at 2m/s. Perfect for high volume DUPRO applications such as battery production and Food processing.
