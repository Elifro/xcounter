A Global Leader in X-ray Detectors

Headquartered near Stockholm, Sweden with production facilities in the UK and Finland, the XCounter group is the largest manufacturer of Cadmium Telluride detectors in the world.

Over the years we have provided detectors into imaging systems that are used across the globe by a broad range of customers; systems used by dentists, radiographers, surgeons, engineers, food scientists, nuclear technologists, the list of our customers continues to grow.

Quite simply, where any application requires the highest resolution X-ray images as quickly as possible, our detectors provide the solutions.

Knowledge is at the Heart of our Values

With our direct conversion technology, our in-house software development and medical device standard manufacturing, our photon counting X-ray detectors provide market leading Dynamic Range, near perfect spatial resolution, absolute co-registered Dual Energy capability and the highest performance in MTF and DQE. But we are much more than our detectors.

Our research and development team work with some of the best research organisations in the world to keep our photon counting technology at the forefront of X-ray imaging. Our in-house IP in software and hardware not only provides market leading performance in X-ray detectors, it is our platform for delivering bespoke solutions to OEM customers.

An Established Partner

Our manufacturing capability to ISO13485 medical devices standard, along with producing our core technology in one of the best clean rooms in Scandinavia provides our customers with complete confidence in our product and solution reliability.

Most importantly, our successful history has been grounded in our unwavering focus on driving our science forward. Not only do we pride ourselves on presenting the market with the latest in photon counting X-ray detection. We strive to develop and bring to market new solutions. XCounter. Photon counting. ISO Certificated Business

ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 13485:2012 is the established, international quality-management framework that enables monitoring and continual improvement of the way XCounter operates

Our Value of Delivering Quality

ISO 9001:2008 is an established, international quality-management framework. It enables monitoring and continuous improvement of the way XCounter operates, whether that means reaching out to new or strengthening existing partnership agreements, driving down costs, managing risk more effectively or improving staff satisfaction. In short, the ISO 9001:2008 status recognises the high quality of XCounter's comprehensive business processes. ISO 13485:2012 sets internationally recognized standards ensuring a management system for the design and manufacture of medical devices. Also, it constitutes a key requirement for gaining regulatory approval for medical devices in major commercial markets, including the USA.

It is a true measure of any company to be ISO certified, particularly for a medical-device company such as XCounter. ISO certification is required by partners and purchasers of high-end technology of global interest. Thus, XCounter's ISO accreditation marks a key step forward in the commercialisation process of the company's proprietary X-ray technologies. Also, the ISO certification makes up an important step in the company's internal development, reinforcing the commitment to developing ground-breaking applications that deliver high levels of accuracy and precision. In addition, the ISO accreditation makes up an endorsement of Xcounter's high-quality intellectual capital and professionalism, which benefit all of the company's stakeholders.

Accreditation Processes

XCounter's key processes are certified by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) and operates under ISO 9001:2008 as well as ISO 13485:2012. The company's ISO-certification status demonstrates that it meets an international set of standards and guidelines that are becoming vital components fÃ¶r conducting business in the global marketplace.

About ISO Certification

Based in Switzerland, the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) is a global network that identifies what international standards are required by business, government and society. In XCounter's case, the two ISO ceritfications follow a stringent evaluation process conducted by the Swedish Standards Institute (SIS). SIS is the centre for work on standards in Sweden and a working partner in the European (CEN) and global (ISO) networks.
