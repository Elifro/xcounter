HYDRA FX-Series

Technology

The Hydra range of detectors are a direct-digital, dual-energy, photon-counting x-ray detector. Developed around XCounter's powerful new CdTe-CMOS sensor, the Hydra delivers a wide range of capabilities across a broad range of x-ray energy energies. The Hydra sensor provides outstanding imaging in either frame or time-delayed summation (TDS) mode, with a scan speed of >20000 fps.

Dual-energy acquisition with anti-coincidence technology is a key factor in the advanced capabilities of the Hydra sensor. During dual-energy acquisition, the energy of the detected photon is compared to two independent thresholds that are read out separately. The two energy sets can be used for material differentiation, opening the door to new imaging techniques in both medical and industrial x-ray. Anti-coincidence technology produces superior energy resolution by ensuring the attribution of each single photon signal to the correct pixel

Application

The longest length detector at 50cm and the fastest detector at 20,000fps which can detect samples passing at 2m/s. Perfect for high volume DUPRO applications such as battery production and Food processing. It is also the most versatile of our detectors. With its 3 side buttable capability, the hydra can be configured to different image dimensions, where a maximum of 8 Hydra boards can be stacked together in one or two rows to build large detectors according to your needs.

Integration

The Hydra sensor is connected to the computer via a GigE interface. The detector comes with a fully flexible software development kit which runs on Windows XP, Vista and Windows 7. The detector is bundled with engineering software which exposes the various functionalities available.
