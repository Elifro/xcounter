OEM

Technology

XCounter has created a new generation of photon counting energy discriminating detectors. The sensor boards are modular and can be tiled to form lengths of up to 900mm. This means large active area photon counting is a reality. The sensors are designed for very fast scan times and for a large range of energies from 10keV to 225keV. They employ CdTe as the converter which is radiation hard and proven to be robust at extremes of operating environment.

The sensor is the future building block of high resolution direct conversion, energy discriminating, photon counting X-ray detection.

Design

With the same core technology of direct conversion CdTe CMOS detection in all our products, we are able to custom any of our detectors to suit your system requirements. Our in-house world class knowledge of photon counting detection hardware and the processing and imaging software that we design is an opportunity for our customers to fulfil their specific OEM needs. The full range of detector capabilities from ultra-fast 20,000fps to small field of view is available to configure without compromise on MTF and DQE performance. And the flexibility brought by the adaptability of geometric configuration is a powerful tool in providing bespoke OEM solutions. We pride ourselves on the broad scientific and technological expertise within our organisation.

With world respected experts across a large range of topics including firmware, semi-conductor technology, mathematics, quantum physics, medical physics and imaging, we believe that if it can be done with photon counting, then we can do it. And our work and close relationship with research establishments around the world ensure that we bring the latest in detector developments to the market.

Development

Producing 30,000 CdTe CMOS sensors every year for the OEM market, the XCounter group continues to prove its success in supplying significant customers with OEM detector solutions in different markets. Our team have the experience to work with you to address all aspects of the OEM supply chain. Our research and development team work closely with our production team to de-risk component solutions, recognising not only the importance of product performance and reliability, but also that our OEM solutions support our customers brand image. This importance we place on our ISO13485 and ISO9001 quality systems are a measure of our commitment to providing repeatable excellence in product and documentation quality.

Implementation

Our commercial team have facilitated and implemented OEM frameworks in a range of industries across the globe. Their expertise will ensure seamless development of an OEM project, from initial feasibility through to pre-production. Once an OEM supply agreement is in place our production and operations team have expertise in efficiently managing customer relationships, ranging from Key Performance Indicator management, to JIT delivery scheduling.
