Helping Security keep us Safe

Staying at the forefront of technology provides a significant edge to the security industry. Be it homeland security, commercial security, freight and transportation checking or military checkpoint, whatever the application, being able to have reliable, accurate data fast in order to make decisions quickly is essential. Generating a highly accurate X-ray image in as short a space of time as possible supports security professionals in the difficult process of taking appropriate actions quickly. XCounter detectors are redefining the speeds at which X-ray detectors can generate high resolution images, with frame rates of up to 20,000fps. A fast snapshot with incredible detail can be valuable to any security imaging application.

The full product range of XCounter detectors have dual energy capability grounded in cutting edge pulse-height photon energy equivalence. During dual energy acquisition, the energy of the detected photon is compared to two independent thresholds that are read out separately. The two energy sets then give ground breaking material separation information providing you with one of the most powerful material differentiation X-ray detectors available.

And for low dose personnel security scanning, the sensitivity of our detectors provide high quality images for the lowest possible radiation exposure. With our established track record of manufacturing detectors to ISO13485 for the medical industry, XCounter has a profound depth of understanding in delivering low dose X-ray imaging.

Unparalleled Performance

With Detector Quantum Efficiency (DQE) of 85% @ RQA5 spectra and a Modulation Transfer Function (MTF) of >80% @ 2lp/mm, our detectors provide images of the highest spatial resolution. And with our direct conversion CdTe CMOS technology in our solutions, you will not suffer from ghosting, lag issues or blurring at high energies that is often found in ordinary TFT based flat panel detectors.

Our unparalleled Dynamic range gives the opportunity so see all of an object in one exposure and presents more data for analysis. This ability to observe both objects of a broad range of densities in the same exposure provides powerful analysis in identifying contraband and explosives.

In time delay summation (TDS) modes and superfast frame rates, our detectors deliver faster scan times enabling quick throughput imaging. Imaging objects passing the detector at 2m/s is possible. This speed along with high end resolution, contrast and sensitivity of the core direct conversion technology, is further enhanced with proprietary charge sharing correction (CSC) software. Such powerful performance brings new levels of image accuracy and material discernment, helping the security industry “find” objects in the most obscure of hiding places.

Technology

XCounter has a solid foundation in both integrating and photon counting state of the art x-ray detector technology.

The basic component in our detectors are a conversion material, which converts the x-rays into electrical signals, and a CMOS (ASIC) which transforms the electric signals into a datastream of either the integrated electric charge or the number of photons which has entered each pixel. This data is then used to create a digital image which is either two- or threedimensional, depending on the scan geometry.

Our solid state detector uses CdTe which is a direct conversion material. When an x-ray photon enters the material it is converted directly to an electric signal which is captured, measured and finally processed into an image. This provides significantly better MTF (Modulation Transfer Function) and DQE (Detection Quantum Efficiency) compared to scintillation detectors due to the high sensitivity and very good stopping power for high energy photons. By combining the CdTe conversion layer with our custom ASICs (CMOS) we can supply detectors that support integrating as well as photon counting modes, fast readout and low power consumption.
