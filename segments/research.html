Meeting the Demands of Research

The history of XCounter is based in research.

This heritage forms a core value of our business. We continually strive to have cutting edge X-ray detector experts in our team. We proactively bring the best scientist and engineers into our organisation in order to keep pushing forward the boundaries of what can be achieved with X-ray detector technology. This keeps our products at the forefront of what is commercially available. And with the experience of producing over 30,000 hybrid CdTe CMOS chips every year, the XCounter group have are singularly placed at the forefront of direct conversion X-ray detector technology.

With this depth of knowledge XCounter works with leading research establishments around the world, not only to create bespoke detector solutions for unique projects, but also to provide support and guidance on how to get the most out of our detectors for your work.

With the ownership of the Intellectual Property on the core technology of our detectors, along with proprietary software, we are best placed to develop specially configured solutions to add value to research applications. And with most of our scientists from a postgraduate background, we are able to advise on how to configure our existing product range to deliver the results your need in the unique dynamics and pressures of a research environment.

Performance to Support Cutting Edge Science

Quite simply XCounter detectors deliver market leading performance in speed, resolution, sensitivity and contrast. This powerful capability is brought to diverse ground breaking research and development projects across the globe; from synchrotron work to the latest in phase contrast imaging; from examination of X-ray source parameters to microscopic material interface characterisation.

All of our detectors deliver Quantum Efficiency (DQE) of 85% @ RQA5 spectra and a Modulation Transfer Function (MTF) of >80% @ 2lp/mm. And with Charge Sharing Correction (CSC) technology and superfast frame rates of up to 20,000 fps, our off the shelf products can readily bring detector improvements to your research project.

Adaptability both in geometry of active detector area and the ability to configure our software means that our detector solutions can be modified to fit your requirement. And our development scientists are on hand to support you in getting the most from all of this flexibility.

With zero dark signal and zero readout noise, our detectors are their ability to count single photons achieve excellent signal-to-noise ratios. And with large maximum count rates of photons per pixel our detectors are perfect for the high flux environment of synchrotron light sources.

Technology

XCounter has a solid foundation in both integrating and photon counting state of the art x-ray detector technology.

The basic component in our detectors are a conversion material, which converts the x-rays into electrical signals, and a CMOS (ASIC) which transforms the electric signals into a datastream of either the integrated electric charge or the number of photons which has entered each pixel. This data is then used to create a digital image which is either two- or threedimensional, depending on the scan geometry.

Our solid state detector uses CdTe which is a direct conversion material. When an x-ray photon enters the material it is converted directly to an electric signal which is captured, measured and finally processed into an image. This provides significantly better MTF (Modulation Transfer Function) and DQE (Detection Quantum Efficiency) compared to scintillation detectors due to the high sensitivity and very good stopping power for high energy photons. By combining the CdTe conversion layer with our custom ASICs (CMOS) we can supply detectors that support integrating as well as photon counting modes, fast readout and low power consumption.
