XCounter at ECR2016

March 2016

In another successful show, XCounter had a booth at the annual congress of the European Society of Radiology (ECR2016) in Vienna. Continuing to lead the way in bringing Photon Counting technology to the market, XCounter received significant interest from all aspects of the Medical imaging industry. The low dose capability of its detectors was particularly well received, with both radiologists and medical systems manufacturers impressed by the quality of the images generated at up to 60% lower dose than standard detectors.

XCounter have appointed Lind as distributor for China

January 2016

XCounter have appointed Lind as distributor for China as part of its continued growth in exporting its detectors. Lind have a track record of success in bringing foreign technology in X-ray systems to the Chinese market, having worked since 1993 with several leading manufacturers such as Gulmay, Faxitron, ProCon and others. With strong ties in both the NDT and Medical market, Lind provide the opportunity to bring the latest in Photon counting detector technology across a range of industry applications. “I am pleased to make this announcement, which consolidates the work we have done with Lind over the past year. I am excited about the forward relationship with a company that has a strong market presence in China and a reputation of service with both their customers and also their non-Chinese distributor partners,” says Nathanael Allison, Sales and Marketing Director for XCounter.

With a training development programme already in place, Lind will also be able to provide local service support for their customers as part of the agreement.

XCounter at JCHSIP2015 Japan

November 2015

As part of keeping at the forefront of imaging technology, XCounter’s XC-Flite X1 was presented at the recent JCHSIP2015 (Japan Congress on High-Speed Imaging and Photonics) in Morioka, Japan. At the event XCounter and its partners, along with respected academics from Iwate Medical University provided the specialized scientific attendees with information and demonstration of the XCounter photon-counting detector.

Latest Developments with XC-DR450

November 2015

XCounter has upgraded its test C-Arm medical imaging system the XC-DR450, with a customised collimator resulting in significantly reduced dosage levels to achieve high quality images. The imaging system continues to provide a source of development testing for XCounter as well as a demonstration capability for future and current customers. With the current configuration using the Flite FX3 detector system, plans are underway to develop a version with the new Hydra series detectors

Weld Examination Tests with XCounter Detectors Impress at SGS

November 2015

XCounter photon counting technology recently demonstrated its powerful NDT capability at the SGS facility in Madrid. In a range of tests both exotic material welds and standard steel welds were examined achieving impressive images of small items ranging from a 10micron hole on a phantom, to never before seen internal defects in turbine blades.

Visuray

November 2015

One of our key OEM’s in the Oil and Gas sector has successfully completed their first commercial well operation with their latest X-ray wireline technology for downhole imaging. The core of this ground-breaking wireline tool uses bespoke XCounter photon counting detectors to provide highest resolution accurate images in the extremely hostile environment of oil field well. The operation, that was made in Holland, paves the way for a new era in Intelligent Well Intervention.

New Staff recruited in London Office

November 2015

In order to maintain its leading presence in the market place, XCounter continues to invest in bringing the best talent available into its organisation. Three new staff, all leaders in their field, have been recently recruited into the London office. With plans to bring the London facility to full blown production next year, XCounter sees that this increase in staff will continue in the near future.
